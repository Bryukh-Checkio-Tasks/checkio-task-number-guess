Given an unknown number, range from 1 to 100, and your task is, guess the number by doing series of attempts.

Your solution will guess the number by submitting an integer divisor, range from 2 to 10, and your best guess number. At each attempt, you will get information, as a list of tuples, whose each tuple contains its remainder result, and your previous divisor.

You should find the number in 8 guesses.

Input: Information about previous attempt. A list of tuples. Each tuple contains its remainder and previous divisor.

Output: A list of integer divisor and your best guess. A list of two integers.

Example: 

checkio([(1,5)])                # the number has a remainder 1
checkio([(1,5),(2,3)])          # the number has a remainder 2
checkio([(1,5),(2,3),(3,4)])    # the number has a remainder 3
checkio([(1,5),(2,3),(3,4),(3,8)])    # the number has a remainder 3
